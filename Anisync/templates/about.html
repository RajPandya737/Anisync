FAQ

1. How does Anisync work?

Simply tell us your MAL username on our seach page. Our site analyzes your anime list and matches them with Osu! beatmaps within our database, and then displays them to you.

2. My full list is not showing up, why is that?

Due to the way we are currently pulling data from MyAnimeList, themes on your profile can cause issues with our site. If you are using a theme, please disable it and try again. Additional problems include having a private list, or typing your username incorrectly. Currently, we only display your top 300 anime due to API and webscrapping limitations.

3. Is there a cost to use Anisync?

No, we want to give you the best experiance possible and as of right now, we believe keeping this service free is the best scenario for everyone

4. Some shows say unavaliable, why is that?

There are plently of reasons as to why this may show up, as of right now, we do not support most OVA's, Movies, or Specials unless they are widely popular and have a large fanbase. Another possibility is that we could not find a suitable map for the anime in our search. If you would like to suggest a map, please contact us and we will review it (contact details at the bottom of the page).

5. Can I suggest beatmaps?

Absolutely! We encourage community engagement. If you come across an Osu! beatmap that perfectly complements an anime, you can suggest it. We're always excited to expand our collection. For contact details, view the bottom of this page.

6. Is Anisync affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!?

No, Anisync is an independent project created by an anime and Osu! enthusiast. We're not officially affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!, but we're excited to bring their worlds together in a unique way.

7. Is this website safe?

This project is completely safe. We do have Google Analytics running in the background, but that is to just mointor the scale of the website. We are fully open source and you can view all of the code by clicking on the github link at the top of this page.

8. How can I support this project?

The best way to support this project is to share it with your friends and family who are also anime and Osu! fans. Starring the respository on github can also help us reach more people.
