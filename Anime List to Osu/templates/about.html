About

Welcome to Mal to Osu! - Uniting Anime and Rhythm! Are you a passionate anime lover who also enjoys the exhilarating world of rhythm games? If so, you've just stumbled upon your new favorite online destination. Mal to Osu is a unique platform that bridges the gap between your anime adventures and the pulse-pounding excitement of osu! beatmaps. What We Do: As an Osu! player myself, I understand that anime and rhythm have an inexplicable harmony. This platform takes your love for both to the next level. Anime Meets Rhythm: Delve into a collection of handpicked osu! beatmaps that are thematically linked to the anime you've enjoyed. Each beatmap tells a story, mirrors an anime's essence, or echoes its soundtrack, offering an immersive experience like no other. Discover and Play: Explore your personalized list of Osu! beatmaps, ranging from energetic openings to sentimental endings. Join us at Mal to Osu! and embark on a journey that harmonizes the world of anime with the pulsating beats of osu!. It's time to amplify your entertainment experience and immerse yourself in a realm where anime narratives and rhythm unite in spectacular harmony. Your Anime Adventures. Rhythmically Enhanced.

FAQ

1. How does Mal to Osu! work?

Simply input your MAL username on our seach page. Our site analyzes your anime list and matches them with osu! beatmaps within our database, and then displays them to you. Even if the anime does not appear within the database, we will still try to find the closest match we can.

2. Is there a cost to use MAL to Osu?

No, we want to give you the best experiance possible and as of right now, we believe keeping this service free is the best scenario for everyone

3. Some shows say unavaliable, why is that?

There are plently of reasons as to why this may show up, as of right now, we do not support most OVA's, Movies, or Specials at this time unless they are widely popular and have a large fanbase. Another possibility is that we could not find a suitable map for the anime in our search. If you would like to suggest a map, please contact me and we will review it (contact details at the bottom of the page).

4. Can I suggest anime-themed beatmaps?

Absolutely! Weencourage community engagement. If you come across an osu! beatmap that perfectly complements an anime, you can suggest it on our platform. We're always excited to expand our curated collection. For contact details, view the bottom of this page.

5. Is MAL to Osu! affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!?

No, Mal to Osu! is an independent project created by an anime and rhythm enthusiast. We're not officially affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!, but we're excited to bring their worlds together in a unique way.

6. Is this website safe?

This project is completely safe and free from malware or tracking of any sort. We are open source and you can view all of the code by clicking on the github link at the top of this page. The only thing we hide is our database of anime as it takes a lot of time to create that and we would perfer to keep it private for the time being.

7. How can I support this project?

Just use it, we just want people to have a good time playing the game that they like, and using our platform lets us know we are doing a good job. If you want to go above and beyond, you can star the repository on github and share it with some of your friends who also watch anime and love Osu! like us.
