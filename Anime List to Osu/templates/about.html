FAQ

1. How does AniRhythmConnect work?

Simply input your MAL username on our seach page. Our site analyzes your anime list and matches them with osu! beatmaps within our database, and then displays them to you. Even if the anime does not appear within the database, we will still try to find the closest match we can.

2. Is there a cost to use AniRhythmConnect?

No, we want to give you the best experiance possible and as of right now, we believe keeping this service free is the best scenario for everyone

3. Some shows say unavaliable, why is that?

There are plently of reasons as to why this may show up, as of right now, we do not support most OVA's, Movies, or Specials at this time unless they are widely popular and have a large fanbase. Another possibility is that we could not find a suitable map for the anime in our search. If you would like to suggest a map, please contact me and we will review it (contact details at the bottom of the page).

4. Can I suggest beatmaps?

Absolutely! We encourage community engagement. If you come across an osu! beatmap that perfectly complements an anime, you can suggest it on our platform. We're always excited to expand our collection. For contact details, view the bottom of this page.

5. Is AniRhythmConnect affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!?

No, AniRhythmConnect is an independent project created by an anime and Osu! enthusiast. We're not officially affiliated with MyAnimeList or Osu!, but we're excited to bring their worlds together in a unique way.

6. Is this website safe?

This project is completely safe and free from malware or tracking of any sort. We are open source and you can view all of the code by clicking on the github link at the top of this page. The only thing we hide is our database of anime as it takes a lot of time to create that and we would perfer to keep it private for the time being.

7. How can I support this project?

Just use it, we just want people to have a good time playing the game that they like, and using our platform lets us know we are doing a good job. If you want to go above and beyond, you can star the repository on github and share it with some of your friends who also watch anime and love Osu! like us.
